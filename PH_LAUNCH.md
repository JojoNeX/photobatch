## 📝 Description

```
PhotoBatch is a one-click batch photo editor that runs entirely in your browser — no install, no upload, no cloud. Your photos never leave your machine.

✨ WHAT IT DOES:
• Blur faces, license plates, or anything — batch apply across hundreds of photos
• Add watermarks (custom text, adjustable opacity)
• Overlay text annotations
• Resize entire folders at once
• Export to PNG, JPEG, WebP — quality control included

💡 WHY I BUILT IT:
I was tired of opening Photoshop just to blur 200 event photos. It took 45 minutes. With PhotoBatch: 3 clicks, 10 seconds. Done.

💰 PRICING:
• Free: up to 10 photos, 2 blur zones, trial watermark
• Premium: 19€ lifetime — unlimited everything, no watermark, custom export

No subscription. No cloud lock-in. One file. Lifetime access.
```
